3. How to use our AI for speech enhancement

1

Listening

Our AI-driven listening comprehension module begins by playing an audio recording of a spoken sentence or passage to the user. Subsequently, it utilizes cutting-edge speech recognition capabilities to accurately transcribe the spoken content into text. Once the transcription is generated, the system presents questions or prompts related to the audio content, requiring the user to comprehend and respond appropriately. The user's responses are then evaluated based on their accuracy and relevance to the original audio, providing valuable feedback to enhance listening skills.

2

Speaking

Our AI-powered speech evaluation system operates by first recording the user's spoken sentence. Next, it utilizes advanced speech recognition technology to accurately transcribe the spoken words into text format. Once the transcription is complete, the system assesses the pronunciation and fluency of the sentence by comparing it against predefined linguistic criteria or through machine learning algorithms trained on native speaker data. Finally, it provides the user with a comprehensive score or feedback based on various aspects such as pronunciation accuracy, intonation, and clarity, aiding them in refining their speaking skills effectively.

3

Writing

In the writing exercise, users are presented with a photograph and are asked to describe it in written form. They carefully observe the details and elements within the image, such as objects, people, scenery, colors, and textures. Then, they use their linguistic skills and creativity to craft a descriptive passage that effectively captures the essence of the photograph. This exercise encourages users to pay close attention to visual cues, develop their vocabulary, and practice their ability to convey visual information through written language.
